Services

Microsoft is pleased to have been involved in the discussions with the Department of Financial Services under the Ministry of Finance, Government of India, the Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India (IRDAI) and the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) in order to create awareness of cloud technologies and understand their concerns. We are also delighted to have helped a number of financial institutions in India to move to the cloud. Whether it is State Bank of India which has adopting Office365 to to drive communication and collaboration among its employees, YES Bank relying on Kaizala solutions to establish enabling digital communication channels over a familiar chat-driven interface, Jamia Co-operative Bank, which is using Microsoft Azure to offer the kind of services and digital experiences that customers might expect from much larger banks,
or Kotak Mahindra Bank, which is using Microsoft Azure to achieve efficiency and cost savings without compromising security for its HR operations, financial institutions are benefiting from cloud services to enhance operations and provide better services to their customers.
